FAQ

What’s the difference between the services?
One-Page Launch is a custom one-page site with 1 light revision. Three-Page Site is a custom three-page site with 2 light revisions. 1-Month Unlimited is 30 calendar days of direct work for one business.
What can be requested?
1-Month Unlimited includes unlimited requests including: Websites, Refinements on Existing Content, AI agents, Integrations, Branding, Data Analysis, Bottleneck Breakouts, Marketing Materials, Services, Graphic design, Dashboards, Web applications, and more.
How fast is delivery?
When a request goes active, it gets a stated delivery window. Larger requests ship in checkpoints until complete.
How do requests work?
For 1-Month Unlimited, unlimited requests can be queued. One request is active at a time, if it’s something smaller, at times I may have the room to double-up, but not always.
How do revisions work?
One-Page Launch includes 1 light revision. Three-Page Site includes 2 light revisions. 1-Month Unlimited includes unlimited revisions during the active month.
What is a light revision?
One consolidated round submitted within 7 calendar days of delivery.
How does onboarding work?
A short alignment exchange by email: goals, priority, links/assets, and what “done” means to you for any given request. Then I get to work, and send you the results, and we go from there.
How fast do you start?
All payments are taken upfront. Work begins the same day payment clears.
How do we communicate?
Call, Email, Text, whatever is easiest for you. A shared board can be added. Something like private ChatGPT space can be included.
Do you use AI?
Yes. AI is part of my workflow across web services, with a heavy human hand & eye in the loop.
Do you build in Webflow?
Not at the moment. Looking for a good easy fit for both myself and my clients.
How many people from my team can be involved?
Unlimited participants. One designated approver signs off on final decisions.
What about images and visual assets?
Brand-matched visuals can be created and tailored to your style. Third-party assets can be licensed for your use, with the license details passed through.
How does hosting work?
Hosting is included with 1-Month Unlimited while active, upon request. Hosting is available separately for One-Page Launch and Three-Page Site at $60/mo per site.
What happens outside the selected package?
Additional work beyond the selected package is billed at $75/hr.
What’s the refund policy?
1-Month Unlimited is month-to-month. Cancel anytime. It includes a 1-week 75% money-back guarantee. After the first week, the month remains active through day 30 and is non-refundable. All other work is non-refundable.
